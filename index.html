ERC Newsletter Builder

Build and export the Education Research Center newsletter in 5 steps.

  1. 1. Upload
  2. 2. Triage
  3. 3. Preview
  4. 4. Edit
  5. 5. Export

Step 1: Upload Content

Paste or upload your newsletter markdown draft here. This step will let you load raw content for processing.

Step 2: Triage Sections

Review and organize the parsed sections. Reorder, enable, or disable each section before generating the final newsletter.

Step 3: Preview

See a live preview of the rendered newsletter HTML. Check layout and content before making edits.

Step 4: Edit

Make fine-grained edits to individual sections, items, and metadata before exporting.

Step 5: Export

Copy the finished HTML to clipboard or download it as a file, ready to paste into Outlook Web App.
